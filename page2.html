Characters of Noli Me Tangere

Each character in the book of Noli Me Tangere, by Dr. Jose Rizal, leaves a huge significance throughout the plot. The table below elaborates some important characters, to leave a better understanding for the readers.

Character Description
Crisostomo Ibarra
  • Crisostomo Ibarra is known as the handsome young son
    of Don Rafael Ibarra, one of the richest men in the city of San Diego
  • At least half of his lifetime is spent in another country,
    due to his parent's wish for him to study abroad
  • He is now engaged to the beautiful flawless young lady, Maria Clara.
    A perfect fit for his good-looking physique.
Maria Clara
  • Maria Clara is the adopted daugther of Kapitan Tiago
    and Donya Pia Alba.
  • She is known as the "meztisang Filipina" because of her fair skin,
    healthy hair, and glowing smile.
Kapitan Tiago
  • Kapitan Tiago has one of the highest positions
    in the province of San Digeo.
  • Physically, he was described as someone who is short,
    but has western-like features.
Padre Damaso
  • Padre Damaso appears to have an intimidating
    and arrogant aura. He acts as if he is superior compared to the other
    characters, and doesn't agree to the idea of receiving
    education from another country.
Tiya Isabel
  • Tiya Isabel is the cousin of Kapitan Tiago and care-taker of Maria Clara.
  • Ever since the death of Donya Pia Alba, she has taken the role as Maria
    Clara's Mother Figure.
  • She is known to be someone who is religious, kind, and loyal.
Basilio
  • Basilio is the older brother of Crispin and first child of Sisa.
  • He is a caring and a consistent worker as a Sacristan in the church.
Crispin
  • Just like his brother, Crispin also works as a Sacristan in the Church
  • He is the second child of Sisa, and youngest brother of Basilio
Sisa
  • Sisa is the mother of Basilio and Crispin
  • She is described as a mother who is willing to do anything for both
    of her children, even if it meant hurting herself.
Pilosopo Tasyo
  • Pilosopo Tasyo is known to be intelligent but "crazy"
  • He is a well-educated person when he was younger, but he was
    forced to stop studying - leading him to marriage.
Sepulturero
  • This Sepulturero was the person Ibarra encountered when
    he came to visit his dead father.
  • He shows a great symbolism of what society was like back then,
    indicating societal issues such as forced labor.

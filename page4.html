WHO IS BELLA HADID?
  • Isabella Khair Hadid, mostly known as "Bella Hadid" is a significant American fashion model in modern day's fashion industry. At the young age of 16, her modeling career made its' way to the IMG Models somewhere between August of 2014.

  • The model was born on the 9th of October, 1996. Her father is Mohamed Hadid, who is a Palestinian real estate developer. On the other hand -- her mother, Yolanda Hadid, is a former Dutch model.




    • HER SIGNIFICANT MOVEMENTS
  • Unlike other famous figures in the fashion industry, Bella Hadid raises awareness towards the genocide happening between Palestine and Israel. She was proud enough to voice out and use her platform to educate those who are not aware towards the relevant situation.

  • On one of her instagram posts, I quote, "Wars have laws - and they must be upheld, no matter what. We need to keep pressure on our leaders wherever we are, not to forget the urgent needs of the people of Gaza, and to ensure that innocent Palestinian civilians are not forgotten casualites of this war."








    • IMPACT TOWARDS THE YOUTH
  • Hadid openly shares her experiences and opinions towards sensitive topics such as raising mental health awareness, responding to global disasters, voicing out animal rights, and so much more.

  • Back in the year 2022 in October, the model received the Michael Kors Award for Mental Health Advocacy at the Golden Hearts Awards - a fundraiser host that provides meals to people who struggle with their mental healths.


    • PROFESSIONALISM TOWARDS MODELING
  • Bella, together with her sister Gigi, is known for her breath-taking walks during her runway shows. The model perfectly showcased her mastery towards different techniques while on stage, aside from the common catwalk.
